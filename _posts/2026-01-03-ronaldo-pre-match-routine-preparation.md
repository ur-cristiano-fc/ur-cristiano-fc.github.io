A: Before a match, Ronaldo focuses on a diet rich in complex carbohydrates and lean protein. This typically includes foods like pasta, rice, chicken, fish (especially cod or tuna), eggs, and plenty of fresh fruits and vegetables. He avoids sugary drinks, processed foods, and anything high in fat.

### Q: Does Ronaldo listen to music before games?

A: Yes, like many athletes, Ronaldo often listens to music as part of his **mental preparation** and **music for motivation**. It helps him focus, block out distractions, and get into the right mindset before stepping onto the pitch. He often wears headphones when **arriving at stadium**.
