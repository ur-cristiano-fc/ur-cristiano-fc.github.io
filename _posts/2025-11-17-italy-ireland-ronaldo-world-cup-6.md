## Why Is Cristiano Ronaldo Set for a Record 6th World Cup?

Cristiano Ronaldo is expected to reach his **sixth World Cup** because he remains an active part of Portugal's national squad, continues to deliver strong performances, and has consistently participated in every edition since 2006. If Portugal qualifies, he will become the first male footballer to play in six World Cups.

## Italy in Trouble Again — Why They Are at Risk

Italy, the four-time World Cup champions, are once again struggling in the qualification rounds. After missing World Cup 2018 and failing to qualify directly in 2022, fans worry history may repeat.

### Key Reasons for Italy’s Danger

* Unstable attacking structure
* Poor finishing in critical matches
* Defensive errors in late-game situations
* Injuries to key players

### Italy's Current Standing

Italy are stuck battling in a competitive group and must win their final fixtures or face another playoff situation.
