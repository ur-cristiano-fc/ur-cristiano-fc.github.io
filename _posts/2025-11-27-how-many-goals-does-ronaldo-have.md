# Why Statistics Vary: Understanding the Discrepancies

Because different organizations, websites, and record-keepers use slightly different criteria (e.g., including/excluding friendlies, different competitions, date of last update), you’ll often see small variations when searching “how many goals does Ronaldo have”. That’s why some sources list **950**, some **937**, some **942**, etc. 

Important factors:

- Official vs. unofficial matches  
- Friendlies vs. competitive games  
- Updates as of particular dates  
- Inclusion of club + international goals  

Therefore, when quoting numbers, it’s best to mention **approximate** values or specify the date of update.
