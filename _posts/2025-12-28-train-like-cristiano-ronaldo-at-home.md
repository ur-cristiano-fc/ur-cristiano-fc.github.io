## Step-by-Step: Train Like Cristiano Ronaldo at Home

### Step 1: Warm-Up (10 Minutes)

A proper warm-up prevents injuries and improves performance.

Warm-up routine:

* Jumping jacks – 2 minutes
* High knees – 1 minute
* Arm circles – 1 minute
* Dynamic leg swings – 2 minutes
* Light jogging in place – 4 minutes

---

### Step 2: Core Workout (Ronaldo’s Secret Weapon)

Ronaldo’s core strength helps him shoot, jump, and change direction quickly.

Core routine:

* Plank – 3 sets of 60 seconds
* Side plank – 3 sets each side
* Bicycle crunches – 3 sets of 25 reps
* Russian twists – 3 sets of 30 reps

---

### Step 3: Leg Day (Focus on Ronaldo Calves)

Ronaldo’s calves are famous for their size and definition.

Leg and calves workout:

* Squats – 4 sets of 20 reps
* Lunges – 3 sets of 15 reps per leg
* Jump squats – 3 sets of 15 reps
* Calf raises – 5 sets of 30 reps
* Single-leg calf raises – 3 sets per leg

This routine helps develop explosive leg power.

---

### Step 4: Upper Body Strength

Ronaldo’s muscles are balanced, not bulky.

Upper body workout:

* Push-ups – 4 sets of 25 reps
* Diamond push-ups – 3 sets of 15 reps
* Dips using a chair – 3 sets of 15 reps
* Pike push-ups – 3 sets of 12 reps

---

### Step 5: Cardio Like Ronaldo

Ronaldo combines endurance and speed.

At-home cardio routine:

* Sprint in place – 30 seconds
* Rest – 30 seconds
* Repeat for 15 rounds

Alternative options:

* Skipping rope
* Stair running
* Shadow sprinting
