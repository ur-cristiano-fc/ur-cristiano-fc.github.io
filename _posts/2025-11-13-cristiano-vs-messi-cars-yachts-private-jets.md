## Yachts: Floating Palaces of the Football Kings

### Cristiano Ronaldo’s Yacht Collection

Cristiano Ronaldo owns a luxurious **Azimut-Benetti yacht**, worth **$7 million**, which he uses for Mediterranean vacations with his family. The 88-foot vessel includes:

* 5 cabins and a modern kitchen
* Lounge deck with Jacuzzi
* Full-speed range of 30 knots

Ronaldo personally custom-designed parts of his yacht interior, reflecting his refined lifestyle and passion for detail.

### Lionel Messi’s Yacht

Messi owns the **Maiora Seven C**, a **$3 million** yacht that accommodates up to 10 people. It’s equipped with:

* Jet skis and waterboards
* 4 cabins and a sundeck
* 24-meter sleek Italian design

Messi often spends his summers cruising around Ibiza and the Balearic Islands, giving fans glimpses of his family vacations.

## Private Jets: Flying in Style

### Cristiano Ronaldo’s Jet

Ronaldo’s **Gulfstream G650**, valued at **$65 million**, is among the **most expensive private jets owned by any athlete**. Key features include:

* Capacity: 18 passengers
* Speed: Mach 0.925
* Custom CR7 interior branding
* Entertainment and fitness space onboard

### Lionel Messi’s Jet

Messi’s **Gulfstream V**, worth **$50 million**, offers similar luxury but with a softer family touch.

* Capacity: 16 passengers
* Personalized details, including the names of his wife and children on the tail
* Features: Two bathrooms, kitchen, and comfortable sleeping areas

Both jets reflect their owners’ distinct personalities — Ronaldo’s sleek and modern versus Messi’s warm and family-oriented.
