## What Exactly Cristiano Ronaldo Said About the World Cup

During the discussion, Piers Morgan asked Cristiano Ronaldo about his ambitions regarding the World Cup. Ronaldo did not hesitate in his response.

He said that **winning the World Cup is “not a dream”** for him. Instead, it is a **goal**.

This difference between *dream* and *goal* is important:
- A dream implies something distant, perhaps unreachable.
- A goal implies direction, planning, commitment, and belief.

Ronaldo emphasized that he approaches success with intention, not fantasy. His journey has not been based on waiting for opportunities; it has been based on pursuing them relentlessly.

This statement reflected the same mindset that shaped his career:
- The belief that hard work turns ambition into achievement.
- The refusal to see greatness as something only imagined.
- The continuous drive for excellence even at an age when many players slow down.
