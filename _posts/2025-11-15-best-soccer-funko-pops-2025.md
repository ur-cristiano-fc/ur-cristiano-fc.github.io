## Why Is There No Cristiano Ronaldo Funko Pop?

**Short Answer:** Cristiano Ronaldo has no Funko Pop figure in 2025 due to licensing restrictions, his personal brand agreements, and complex image rights involving multiple clubs and sponsorships.

This prevents Funko from producing an approved CR7 figure despite massive fan demand.
