#### What Makes a Diamond Ring Special?

A **diamond ring** isn't just about how big it is; it's also about its quality. Experts often talk about the "Four Cs" when judging a diamond:

*   **Carat:** This is how heavy the diamond is, and usually, bigger carats mean bigger diamonds.
*   **Cut:** How well the diamond has been shaped and polished, which affects its sparkle. A good cut makes a diamond shine more.
*   **Clarity:** How few tiny marks or spots (called inclusions) are inside the diamond. The fewer, the better.
*   **Color:** How colorless the diamond is. The less color, the more valuable it usually is.

You can learn more about diamond quality and the "Four Cs" from reputable jewelers like [Tiffany & Co.](https://www.tiffany.com/engagement/the-tiffany-guide-to-diamonds/the-4cs-of-a-diamond/) or [De Beers](https://www.debeers.com/en-us/the-home-of-diamonds/the-4cs/).
